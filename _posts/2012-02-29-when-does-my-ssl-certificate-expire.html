Quick and dirty method for checking the expiration data on an SSL certificate:

$ echo "" | openssl s_client -connect paul.ouderkirk.ca:443 2> /dev/null | openssl x509 -noout -enddate
notAfter=Dec 29 05:00:47 2012 GMT
